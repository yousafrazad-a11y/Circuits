# 5-Way Joint Intersection vs Pairwise Global Intersection

This analysis compares the **5-Way Global Circuit** (generated by training all 5 datasets simultaneously and branching into 5 specific circuits) against the **Pairwise Global Circuit** (generated by training 10 specific pairs and finding the global intersection of their intersections).

## Circuit Similarity

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| **Size (5-Way Circuit)** | 45 heads |
| **Size (Pairwise Global Circuit)** | 27 heads |
| **Intersection Size** | 25 heads |
| **Union Size** | 47 heads |
| **Jaccard Similarity** | 0.5319 |
| **Overlap (5-Way inside Pairwise)** | 0.5556 |
| **Overlap (Pairwise inside 5-Way)** | 0.9259 |

## Analysis
The pipelines generated highly similar, but distinct circuits.
